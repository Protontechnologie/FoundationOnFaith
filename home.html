When you’re starting over with nothing…

People don’t deserve a life sentence for mistakes as common as trusting the wrong person or being born in the wrong socioeconomic class. We believe that access to knowledge and resources can change – and save – lives. It’s our duty to bring those resources to the people who won’t take them for granted.

The team behind the dream

Founding on Faith by CPS is an entrepreneur-led organization of experienced and compassionate professionals. Having leaned heavily on faith to withstand her own storm, our Founder is drawn to the recovery of others.

Get help

If you’re a single parent or a minor without a safe and reliable home, send us a message right now. We’ll meet with you as soon as possible to discuss your situation and bring you to safety and relief.
